## Task
Create a professional consulting proposal outline that clearly defines scope, deliverables, timeline, and pricing for the consulting engagement.

## Engagement Types

### Discovery/Assessment (2-4 weeks)
Focus on problem identification and recommendations

### Implementation (1-6 months)
Focus on executing defined solution

### Retainer/Ongoing (monthly)
Focus on continuous support and optimization

### Training/Workshop (1-5 days)
Focus on knowledge transfer and skill building

## Requirements

### Must Include
1. Executive summary of problem and solution
2. Detailed scope of work with deliverables
3. Phased timeline with milestones
4. Clear pricing structure
5. Key terms and conditions
6. Success criteria
7. Assumptions and constraints
8. Out of scope items

### Must NOT Include
1. Vague or unclear deliverables
2. Unrealistic timelines
3. Scope without boundaries
4. Pricing without structure
5. Promises beyond capability
6. Missing assumptions
7. Unclear responsibilities

### Quality Standards
- Professional, polished presentation
- Clear, specific language
- Realistic commitments
- Fair pricing
- Balanced terms

## Quality Checklist

Before providing output, verify:
- [ ] Scope is clear and achievable
- [ ] Deliverables are specific and measurable
- [ ] Timeline is realistic with buffer
- [ ] Pricing is competitive and fair
- [ ] Terms protect both parties
- [ ] Assumptions are stated
- [ ] Out of scope items prevent scope creep
- [ ] Professional presentation
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] You would accept this if you were the client

## Pricing Guidelines

### Pricing Models
1. **Fixed Price**: Best for well-defined projects
2. **Time & Materials**: Best for uncertain scope
3. **Value-Based**: Best for high-impact outcomes
4. **Retainer**: Best for ongoing relationships

### Rate Considerations
- Your experience level and market rate
- Project complexity and risk
- Client budget constraints
- Relationship and repeat business potential
- Industry standard rates
- Opportunity cost

### Buffer Guidelines
- Add 20-30% time buffer for unknowns
- Price at higher end if scope is uncertain
- Include contingency in phases
- Clear change order process

## Examples

### Discovery Engagement Example

**Investment**: $15,000
**Duration**: 4 weeks
**Deliverables**:
- Current state assessment document
- Recommendations report with priorities
- Implementation roadmap
- Executive presentation

**Payment Terms**: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery

### Implementation Project Example

**Investment**: $45,000
**Duration**: 12 weeks
**Deliverables**:
- Implemented solution
- Documentation
- User training
- 30-day post-launch support

**Payment Terms**: 33% upfront, 33% at 6 weeks, 34% on completion

### Retainer Example

**Investment**: $5,000/month
**Duration**: 6-month minimum
**Deliverables**:
- 20 hours monthly consulting
- Monthly strategy review
- Ongoing optimization
- Ad-hoc support

**Payment Terms**: Monthly in advance

## Notes

- Always under-promise and over-deliver
- Build in buffer for unexpected issues
- Clear scope prevents scope creep
- Multiple pricing options give client choice
- Professional presentation builds confidence
- Terms should be fair to both parties
- Make it easy for client to say yes
