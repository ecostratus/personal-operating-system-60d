### 7. Preparation Checklist

**Before Interview**:
- [ ] Review company website and recent news
- [ ] Read interviewer LinkedIn profiles
- [ ] Practice STAR stories aloud
- [ ] Prepare questions to ask
- [ ] Test tech (if video interview)
- [ ] Plan logistics (what to wear, transportation)
- [ ] Print resume copies (if in-person)
- [ ] Prepare portfolio/work samples (if applicable)

**Mental Preparation**:
- [ ] Visualize successful interview
- [ ] Remember: You're evaluating them too
- [ ] Prepare for nerves (breathing exercises)
- [ ] Get good sleep night before
- [ ] Arrive/log in 10 minutes early

### 8. Interview Day Tips

**Opening**:
- Warm greeting, firm handshake (or friendly demeanor if video)
- Thank them for their time
- Show enthusiasm and energy

**During**:
- Listen carefully to questions
- Take moment to think before answering
- Use STAR structure for behavioral questions
- Ask for clarification if needed
- Show genuine interest and curiosity
- Take notes on their answers

**Closing**:
- Summarize your interest and fit
- Ask about next steps
- Thank them again
- Send thank-you email within 24 hours

## Quality Checklist
