## Quality Checklist

Before completing review, verify:
- [ ] All metrics analyzed
- [ ] Patterns identified
- [ ] Root causes explored, not just symptoms
- [ ] Recommendations are specific and actionable
- [ ] Goals are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- [ ] Sustainability considered
- [ ] Honest assessment, not overly positive or negative

## Review Principles

1. **Be Honest**: Face reality, good or bad
2. **Be Specific**: Vague insights aren't helpful
3. **Be Forward-Looking**: Learn from past, plan for future
4. **Be Balanced**: Celebrate wins, address challenges
5. **Be Actionable**: Every insight should lead to action
6. **Be Sustainable**: Don't overcommit

## Notes

- Weekly reviews should take 30-60 minutes
- Document everything - memory fades
- Look for patterns, not just individual data points
- Adjust strategy based on data, not feelings
- Celebrate progress, even if small
- Be kind to yourself while staying accountable
- Pivot quickly when something isn't working
- Double down on what's working well
