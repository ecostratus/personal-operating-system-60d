# Glossary

## Observation

A raw fact extracted from a source prior to validation or inference.

## Evidence

A validated observation set with source provenance and quality metadata used to support claims.

## Claim

A confidence-scored assertion about a person, opportunity, or context that is grounded in evidence.

## Attribute

A descriptive property of an entity (for example, location, compensation band, or role scope).

## Capability

A demonstrated or inferred ability of the person, derived from one or more claims.

## Policy

A configuration profile that sets optimization intent, weights, thresholds, and constraints.

## Decision

An immutable evaluation artifact generated for a person-opportunity-policy tuple.

## Recommendation

An action outcome derived from a decision (for example, `APPLY_IMMEDIATELY` or `MONITOR`).

## Constraint

A hard rule that can gate or override recommendation outcomes.

## Trajectory

A directional estimate of fit or growth over time based on current signals and policy framing.

## Decision Debt

Accumulated risk from deferred, low-confidence, or under-explained decisions.

## Opportunity

A unit of evaluation representing a potential engagement, with `FTE` and `CONTRACT` in v1.

## Person

The evaluated candidate profile, including claims and capability evidence.

## Domain Pack

A schema-validated bundle of domain-specific mappings, archetypes, and vocabulary loaded by the core model.

## Knowledge Pack

A versioned collection of evidence sources, transformations, and claim-generation rules.
