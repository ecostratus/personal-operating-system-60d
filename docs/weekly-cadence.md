# Weekly Cadence

## Weekly Schedule Overview

This document defines the weekly operating rhythm for the 60-day execution plan.

## Daily Activities

### Morning Routine (15 minutes)
- Review overnight automation results
- Check job discovery pipeline for new opportunities
- Prioritize daily actions based on pipeline status

### Evening Routine (15 minutes)
- Log daily activities and outcomes
- Update tracking spreadsheet
- Queue automation tasks for overnight processing

## Weekly Structure

### Monday: Planning & Setup
- **Activities**:
  - Review weekly goals and priorities
  - Update job discovery criteria if needed
  - Plan outreach targets for the week
- **Outputs**:
  - Weekly plan documented
  - Priorities ranked
  - Automation queue configured

### Tuesday-Thursday: Execution
- **Activities**:
  - Review and customize resumes for priority opportunities
  - Send planned outreach messages
  - Conduct scheduled interviews or calls
  - Develop consulting proposals
- **Outputs**:
  - Applications submitted
  - Outreach messages sent
  - Interviews completed
  - Proposals drafted

### Friday: Review & Adjustment
- **Activities**:
  - Weekly metrics review
  - Analyze success/failure patterns
  - Adjust strategies and scripts
  - Document lessons learned
- **Outputs**:
  - Weekly review report
  - Strategy adjustments documented
  - Updated risk map

### Weekend: Preparation
- **Activities** (Optional):
  - Deep work on consulting proposals
  - Research target companies
  - Interview preparation
  - Skill development

## Monthly Milestones

### End of Month Activities
- Comprehensive metrics review
- Strategy pivot assessment
- Budget and resource review
- Stakeholder communications (if applicable)

## Cadence Adjustments

### High Activity Periods
- Increase daily review time
- Reduce automation scope for quality
- Focus on high-value activities

### Low Activity Periods
- Expand job discovery criteria
- Increase outreach volume
- Invest in skill development
- Refine automation scripts

## Emergency Procedures

### Urgent Opportunities
- Override normal cadence
- Fast-track resume tailoring
- Immediate outreach
- Document deviation and rationale
