# Master Brief

## Purpose

This document serves as the master brief for the 60-day personal operating system, outlining the overall objectives, approach, and success criteria for the execution plan.

## Objectives

### Primary Goals
- Execute a structured 60-day career development and job search plan
- Maintain systematic approach to job discovery, application, and outreach
- Build and maintain consulting funnel in parallel
- Ensure auditability and reversibility of all actions

### Success Criteria
- Daily progress tracking against 60-day timeline
- Measurable activity metrics for each automation stream
- Regular review and adjustment cycles
- Documented decisions and outcomes

## Approach

### Core Principles
1. **Systematic Execution**: Follow defined processes and workflows
2. **Data-Driven Decisions**: Track metrics and adjust based on outcomes
3. **Human Oversight**: Maintain manual review and override capability
4. **Integrated Tools**: Leverage Microsoft 365, Copilot Studio, and Python scripts

## Scope

### In Scope
- Job discovery and application tracking
- Resume tailoring for specific opportunities
- Professional outreach and networking
- Consulting opportunity development
- Interview preparation and tracking

### Out of Scope
- Generic career advice
- Unstructured job search activities
- Non-measurable networking activities

## Timeline

- **Week 1-2**: Setup and calibration
- **Week 3-6**: Active execution and optimization
- **Week 7-8**: Review, adjustment, and scaling
- **Week 9+**: Sustained operation and refinement
