# Consulting Offer Prompt Specification

## Purpose

Define the prompt structure for generating consulting proposals and service offerings.

## Objectives

- Generate professional consulting proposals
- Clearly define scope and deliverables
- Establish realistic timelines and pricing
- Set appropriate terms and conditions
- Maintain professional standards

## Proposal Types

### 1. Discovery/Assessment Engagement
**Duration**: 2-4 weeks
**Focus**: Problem identification and recommendations
**Deliverable**: Assessment report with recommendations

### 2. Implementation Project
**Duration**: 1-6 months
**Focus**: Executing defined solution
**Deliverable**: Implemented solution with documentation

### 3. Retainer/Ongoing Support
**Duration**: Ongoing (monthly)
**Focus**: Continuous support and optimization
**Deliverable**: Regular reviews and improvements

### 4. Training/Workshop
**Duration**: 1-5 days
**Focus**: Knowledge transfer and skill building
**Deliverable**: Trained team with materials

## Prompt Structure

### Input Requirements
1. **Client Context**: Company, industry, size, challenges
2. **Opportunity Description**: Problem to solve or need to address
3. **Your Capabilities**: Relevant skills and experience
4. **Engagement Type**: Which proposal type
5. **Constraints**: Budget hints, timeline, resources

### Output Requirements
1. **Executive Summary**: Problem and proposed solution
2. **Scope of Work**: Detailed deliverables and approach
3. **Timeline**: Phased schedule with milestones
4. **Pricing**: Fee structure and payment terms
5. **Terms**: Key terms and conditions

## Prompt Template

```
You are helping create a professional consulting proposal.

Client Context:
- Company: [Name and industry]
- Size: [Employee count, revenue if known]
- Challenge: [Problem they're facing]

Opportunity:
[Description of consulting opportunity]

Your Capabilities:
[Relevant experience, skills, and past projects]

Engagement Type: [Discovery / Implementation / Retainer / Training]

Constraints:
- Budget: [If known or estimated]
- Timeline: [Desired timeframe]
- Resources: [Team, tools available]

Instructions:
1. Create professional proposal outline
2. Define clear scope and deliverables
3. Establish realistic timeline
4. Provide pricing structure
5. Include key terms
6. Make it actionable and specific

Provide:
- Executive Summary
- Scope of Work
- Timeline with milestones
- Pricing options
- Key terms
```

## Scope Definition

### Must Include
- Specific deliverables (what client receives)
- Activities and approach (how you'll do it)
- Success criteria (how to measure success)
- Assumptions (what must be true)
- Out of scope (what's not included)

### Best Practices
- Be specific, not vague
- Under-promise, over-deliver
- Include buffer in timeline
- Define acceptance criteria
- Clarify responsibilities (yours vs. client's)

## Pricing Guidelines

### Pricing Models
1. **Fixed Price**: For well-defined projects
2. **Time & Materials**: For uncertain scope
3. **Value-Based**: For high-impact outcomes
4. **Retainer**: For ongoing relationships

### Pricing Considerations
- Market rates for your experience level
- Project complexity and risk
- Client budget constraints
- Relationship potential
- Opportunity cost

### Payment Terms
- Milestone-based for projects
- Monthly for retainers
- 50% upfront, 50% on completion for small projects
- Net 30 terms for established relationships

## Risk Management

### Include in Proposal
- Change request process
- Scope creep prevention
- Communication protocol
- Conflict resolution
- Termination terms

### Protect Yourself
- Clear acceptance criteria
- Written approval requirements
- IP and confidentiality terms
- Liability limitations
- Professional insurance mention

## Review Process

### Human Review Checklist
- [ ] Scope is clear and achievable
- [ ] Pricing is realistic and competitive
- [ ] Timeline has appropriate buffer
- [ ] Terms protect both parties
- [ ] Deliverables are specific
- [ ] Professional presentation
- [ ] Error-free writing
- [ ] Would you accept this proposal?

## Deliverable Standards

### Proposal Format
- Executive summary (1 page)
- Detailed scope (2-3 pages)
- Timeline/schedule (visual)
- Pricing (clear breakdown)
- Terms (1-2 pages)
- About you (brief bio/credentials)

### Professional Polish
- Clean, professional design
- Branded if applicable
- Error-free content
- Logical flow
- Easy to understand
- Action-oriented

## Follow-Up Process

### After Sending Proposal
1. Confirm receipt (24 hours)
2. Offer to discuss (week 1)
3. Answer questions promptly
4. Follow up (week 2)
5. Close or gracefully exit

### Negotiation Approach
- Be flexible on terms, not quality
- Offer options (good, better, best)
- Understand their constraints
- Find win-win solutions
- Know your walk-away point
